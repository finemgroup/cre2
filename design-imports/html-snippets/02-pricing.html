Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Prorated charges or credits will be applied automatically to your next billing cycle.

What is included in the white-label option?

The Enterprise white-label feature allows you to replace all Finem branding with your firm's logo, use your brand's color palette across the application, and host the client-facing portals on your own custom domain (e.g., deals.yourfirm.com).

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer custom pricing for academic programs and qualified non-profits. Please contact our sales team to verify your eligibility.
