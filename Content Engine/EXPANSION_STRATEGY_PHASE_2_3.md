## PART 3: TWO-SIDED DATA MARKETPLACE PHASE

### The Opportunity: CompStak 2.0

**What is CompStak?**

CompStak is a **crowdsourced CRE comps database** where:
- ✅ Real estate professionals voluntarily share transaction data
- ✅ Users get access to 200,000+ comparable sales/leases
- ✅ CompStak monetizes by charging brokers $500-2,000/year
- ✅ Funding: Raised venture capital, valued at $50M+
- ✅ Market: Growing 20-30% annually

**Why It Works**:
1. **Data vacuum**: Brokers desperately need reliable comps data
2. **Professional network**: CRE pros already collaborate informally
3. **Reciprocal incentive**: "I share my deal, I get access to yours"
4. **Network effects**: More users = more data = more value

**The Finem Advantage**:
You have:
- ✅ Existing user base (500+ investors in 3 months)
- ✅ Trusted brand (market research authority)
- ✅ Content hook (reports drive adoption)
- ✅ Proprietary transaction database
- ✅ AI integration (can auto-extract comps from reports)
