## 1. Content Engine Executive Takeaway For Sophex

### Best ideas to borrow

1. Interactive evidence-first reports (comparison, heat map, drill-down).
2. Outcome-first hero + segment selector (investor/broker/advisor).
3. Public property/market pages — **public baseline only**.
4. Soft-then-hard lead capture ladder (preview → save → upload → export).
5. Mid-article and progressive CTAs with progressive profiling.
6. Gated export as value exchange.
7. Comparison + regional heat map widgets.
8. Authority labels and proof above fold.
9. Content flywheel → permissioned data flywheel.
10. Analytics loop without send automation.

### Unsafe until gates clear

Auto nurture, CRM sync, syndication of user data, SEO indexing of contributions, exit-intent capture, scraping without license, appraisal-superiority claims, implicit free-tier public contribution, outbound lead scoring, retargeting on contributed intelligence.
