# Sophex Product Boundary And Positioning

**Status:** Product boundary contract  
**Date:** 2026-05-22  
**Implementation status:** `CONTRACT_ONLY`

## Product Boundary

Sophex is a separate public-facing CRE intelligence, valuation, and contribution marketplace. It is aligned with CRE Platform and Finem Fabricator, but it is not a fork of either project and must not casually copy their production truth.

CRE Platform remains the internal governed operating system and evidence substrate. Finem Fabricator remains the workflow, agent, and report-generation factory. Sophex owns the public marketplace UX, contribution terms, report contracts, and external trust boundary.

## Current Status

- Sophex MVP is not a live marketplace yet.
- Sophex has no real database connection yet.
- This setup creates schema contracts, target-model docs, and local static artifacts only.
- No production data is copied from CRE Platform.
- No migration is applied.
- No schema tool is executed against a database.

## Boundary Commitments

- Public baseline facts and private user/org observations may differ.
- Documents are evidence, not automatic truth.
- Chunks and embeddings are retrieval sidecars, not business truth.
- A master comp is a comp candidate/ecosystem, not one giant canonical table.
- Lease economics, tenants, rent schedules, deals, and investment projections are linked structures, not hardcoded columns on one deal row.
- Public/private visibility must be enforced at query/contract level, not UI-only.

## Non-Launch Posture

No public launch, indexing, syndication, export automation, or user-derived-data marketplace launch is authorized until legal, source-use, privacy, field visibility, and contribution terms are approved.
