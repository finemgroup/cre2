# Product Vision

Sophex is a public-facing CRE intelligence marketplace where property research, comps, user-contributed evidence, and valuation/reporting workflows create a defensible data flywheel.

## Core Product

- Public property baseline: property records, acreage, GIS, title/public records, traffic, demographics, zoning, parcel data, and other lawful public datasets.
- Contribution marketplace: users can submit comps, documents, corrections, edits, and private observations.
- Report generation: users can select a property or upload source documents and receive valuation/reporting outputs.
- Permissioned intelligence: public and private values can coexist without flattening all facts into one global truth.
- White-label surface: future partner or broker-branded reporting experiences may be built on the same evidence and permission model.

## Free And Paid Contribution Model

Free valuation or reporting access may be exchanged for user contributions such as source documents, comps, or fact verification. The exchange must be explicit: users should understand what they are contributing, what Sophex may use, what remains private, and what may become public, shared, anonymized, or aggregated.

Paid/private users may upload documents or maintain private property intelligence without automatically exposing those values to the marketplace.

## Data Flywheel

1. Public records provide a baseline property profile.
2. Users upload documents, comps, or corrections in exchange for value.
3. Sophex extracts observations with source, confidence, visibility, and lineage.
4. Report outputs create immediate user value.
5. Verified and permitted observations improve property intelligence.
6. Aggregated and permission-safe intelligence improves future reports, comps, and marketplace depth.

## Moat

The durable advantage is not raw scraping. It is evidence-backed, permission-aware, user-contributed CRE intelligence with auditability, report usefulness, and trusted boundaries between public facts, private facts, and governed CRE substrate data.
